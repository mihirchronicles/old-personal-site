Wise Charlie


Story: Charlie Munger has been a huge source of inspiration to me since I was a teenager. He is known for clear thinking and profound values among many things. He is Warren Buffett's partner-in-crime at Berkshire Hathaway. The concept of mental models was coined by Munger. Mental models are big ideas from big disciplines which can help one think constructively in a multidiscilinary approach. These mental models help you ask the right questions by thinking critically. I wanted to create a central repository of these mental models which would explain them in a fun way.

Product: Wise Charlie is a collection of 100 mental model cards packaged in a beautiful and compact box. It is a pocket tool for your mind. Each model has fun artwork with a short defintion followd by a funny example. I wanted the product to reflect humor because Charlie Munger in real life is hilarious.

Example of a mental model.

Results: The project has been a challenge in ways that I didn't expect when I first started. Manufacturing a physical product is very tough especially when you respect design and quality for your product. Project in alive and in progress. You can visit the project at www.wisecharlie.com.
